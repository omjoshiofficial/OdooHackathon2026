**🚛 TransitOps – Smart Transport Operations Platform**

**Built for Odoo Hackathon 2026**

A modern transport operations platform designed to simplify fleet management, driver operations, trip dispatching, maintenance, fuel tracking, operational expenses, and analytics.

"From manual operations to smart transport management."


**📖 About the Project**

TransitOps is a modern Transport Operations Platform developed as part of the Odoo Hackathon 2026.

Many logistics and transport companies still rely on spreadsheets, manual records, and disconnected systems to manage their daily operations. This often results in scheduling conflicts, underutilized vehicles, delayed maintenance, inaccurate expense tracking, and limited operational visibility.

Our goal was to build a centralized platform that brings all transport operations together into a single, easy-to-use system.

Rather than building individual CRUD pages, we focused on creating a connected workflow where every module works together to improve operational efficiency and reduce manual effort.


**🎯 Problem We Solved**

TransitOps helps organizations manage the complete transport lifecycle by providing a centralized solution for:

1.Fleet Management
2.Driver Management
3.Trip Planning & Dispatch
4.Vehicle Maintenance
5.Fuel Management
6.Expense Tracking
7.Operational Analytics
8.Authentication with RBAC
9.PDF export and CSV export
10.Search, filters, and sorting
11.Dark mode
12.Email reminders for expiring licenses

Our objective was to make transport operations simpler, faster, and more reliable while enforcing real business rules.
